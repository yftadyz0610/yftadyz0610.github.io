About MathAI Lab

The mission of this lab is to fulfill artificial general intelligence (AGI) with using mature machine learning/deep learning techniques and elegant mathematical theories. In recent years, fancy AI algorithms like DeepFM, BERT have greatly boosted industrial applications and created high volume of commercial values. However, the bottleneck of these algorithms has also gradually become clear which results in the founding of this lab. We know this mission is very difficult but we'd like to contribute something to this course. We choose to fight on what's right rather than what's easy.
